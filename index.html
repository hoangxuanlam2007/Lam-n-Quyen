Our Story

The Thirteenth of November

My girlfriend and I are both enrolled in the same class. We started talking to each other on a Discord server, bonding over our shared interests, and then decided it was worth giving a relationship between us a shot. I was a 14-year-old kid about to graduate from secondary school at the time, and she was an adorable girl I'd been pursuing for years.

We had been chatting on Discord for months during quarantines, but it wasn't until that morning when we finally met. We've been together ever since then and now have been together!

A purpose of human life, no matter who is controlling it, is to love whoever is around to be loved.
